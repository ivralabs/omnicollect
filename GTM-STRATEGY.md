## Why OmniCollect Wins Against OMC ROAD

These companies already use OMC ROAD data. The pitch is not "we count cars" — it's "we give you what OMC can't."

| OMC ROAD | OmniCollect |
|---|---|
| Annual survey (7,500 interviews) | Real-time camera data |
| Modelled regional averages | Per-panel actual counts |
| No data 2020–2021 (COVID gap) | Continuous, always-on |
| 1 respondent = ~3,800 people | Every actual vehicle counted |
| No brand vehicle recognition | Brand vehicle recognition (unique in SA) |
| Static estimates | Live VAC, Reach, GRP, CPT per panel |
| No DOOH integration | Cuecast-ready (programmatic ad serving) |

**The positioning line:**
> "OMC tells you the estimate. OmniCollect shows you the truth."

---

## The Cuecast Upsell

For the big players, OmniCollect is the data layer. Cuecast is the revenue layer.

- OmniCollect measures who's in front of each screen, in real time
- Cuecast uses that data to serve the right ad at the right moment — weather-triggered, traffic-triggered, audience-pattern-triggered
- For JCDecaux / Pimedia / Provantage: this is their entry into programmatic DOOH — a global trend they're already investing in
- Selling OmniCollect + Cuecast together = a complete smart billboard platform

## Go-To-Market Phases

### Phase 1 — Prove it works
**Customer:** Box Board Advertising, Gqeberha (Mlu's company)
**Goal:** Real data, real billboards, real case study
**Outcome:** Documented proof — VAC per panel, uptime, accuracy vs OMC estimates

### Phase 2 — Mid-tier SA media owners
**Target:** Companies with 50–500 billboards
- Faster sales cycles
- More willing to try new tech
- Less procurement bureaucracy
- Build MRR + industry credibility

**Sales approach:** Direct outreach, case study from Phase 1, industry events (WOO Africa, OHMSA)

### Phase 3 — Enterprise (JCDecaux, Pimedia, Provantage)
**Timing:** After Phase 2 traction — at least 3–5 mid-tier clients live
**What to bring:**
- Proven case study with real numbers
- OMC-compatible metric outputs (VAC, Reach, Frequency, GRP, CPT)
- Cuecast as the programmatic DOOH upsell
- Enterprise SLA, security review readiness, annual contracts

**Sales approach:** Warm intro where possible (industry events, WOO connections), direct to CTO/Head of Technology or Head of Measurement
