# OmniCollect — Global Expansion Strategy

**Status:** Planning (not building yet)
**Last updated:** 2026-04-18

---

## Market Opportunity

The global OOH advertising market is worth **$40+ billion annually**, growing at ~10% per year. DOOH is the fastest-growing segment. Programmatic DOOH is where every major player globally is investing.

The critical gap: **most markets outside Western Europe and North America have no reliable OOH measurement infrastructure.** They rely on manual counts, rough estimates, and outdated survey models — exactly what OMC ROAD is in SA.

---

## Why OmniCollect is Globally Portable

The core technology works anywhere:
- YOLOv8 vehicle counting — universal
- Brand vehicle recognition — universal
- Mini PC local agent — universal
- Platform, billing model, hardware install approach — universal

**What changes per market:**

| Variable | Adaptation required |
|---|---|
| Number plate formats | Retrain ANPR model per country |
| Vehicle classification | Add local vehicle types (tuk-tuks, matatus, rickshaws, etc.) |
| Measurement standard | Output metrics compatible with local industry body |
| Dashboard language | Localisation |

Everything else is identical. SA is the template.

## Priority Markets

### Tier 1 — Sub-Saharan Africa (expand first)
Highest opportunity, lowest competition, most culturally adjacent to SA.

| Market | Why |
|---|---|
| **Nigeria (Lagos)** | Largest OOH market in Africa, massive billboard estates, zero credible measurement |
| **Kenya (Nairobi)** | Fast-growing economy, active OOH market, tech-forward business culture |
| **Ghana (Accra)** | Stable, growing OOH market, no measurement standard |
| **Uganda (Kampala)** | Emerging OOH market, early mover advantage |

**Advantage:** No Camlytics equivalent in most of these markets. OmniCollect wouldn't be a challenger — it would be the *first* credible measurement tool.

### Tier 2 — Middle East
- Ipsos just acquired Seventh Decimal to crack OOH measurement in GCC (April 2026)
- This validates the market — big money is moving in
- Saudi Arabia, UAE, Egypt are the priority markets
- Competitive but large — Cuecast programmatic layer is the differentiator

### Tier 3 — Southeast Asia & India
- Enormous billboard markets
- Measurement is primitive
- India especially — one of the world's largest OOH markets with almost no real-time data
- Longer-term play — requires more localisation (vehicle types, languages, measurement standards)

### Tier 4 — Latin America
- Large OOH estates in Brazil, Mexico, Colombia
- Limited real-time measurement
- Spanish/Portuguese localisation needed

## The Africa-First Thesis

Africa is the most underserved OOH measurement market in the world — and the most strategically logical expansion from SA:

- Same business culture and market dynamics
- SA case studies carry weight across the continent
- No entrenched competitor to displace
- OOH is growing faster in Africa than anywhere else
- **Long-term vision:** OmniCollect becomes the African OOH measurement currency

This is what Ipsos does globally — license measurement data as an industry currency. OmniCollect can own that position in Africa before any global player moves in.

---

## Long-Term Vision

**Phase 1 — SA** (prove it works)
Box Board → mid-tier SA media owners → JCDecaux / Pimedia / Provantage

**Phase 2 — Africa** (expand the model)
Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana — partner with local media owners, same hardware + software model

**Phase 3 — Programmatic layer** (Cuecast)
OmniCollect data + Cuecast ad serving = end-to-end smart billboard platform
License to media owners across Africa and beyond

**Phase 4 — Data licensing**
Proprietary traffic + audience dataset across African markets
License as measurement currency to agencies, brands, and industry bodies
This is the Ipsos model — OmniCollect becomes infrastructure, not just software

## What Makes This Defensible Globally

By the time OmniCollect reaches international markets:

1. **Proprietary SA dataset** — years of real traffic data, no one can replicate overnight
2. **Brand vehicle recognition** — no global competitor offers this at this price point
3. **OmniCollect + Cuecast bundle** — end-to-end platform, not just a measurement tool
4. **Africa-first positioning** — first mover in the most underserved OOH market globally
5. **OMC + local standard compatibility** — speaks every market's measurement language

## Key Principle

> Prove it in Gqeberha first. One solid SA case study with a major media owner carries more weight in Lagos or Nairobi than any pitch deck.

Don't expand until the SA model is tight, repeatable, and documented. Then it's a copy-paste operation.
